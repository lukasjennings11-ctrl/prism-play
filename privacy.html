Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 18, 2026

PortMaster ("we", "us") is a collection of browser-based arcade games. We take your privacy seriously and collect as little data as possible.

What data we collect

We do not collect any personal information. No accounts, no sign-ups, no tracking cookies.

The following data is stored locally on your device only (via localStorage) and never transmitted to us:

  • High scores and personal bests
  • Daily streak counters
  • Game preferences (sound on/off)

Advertising

The native app versions (iOS and Android) may display ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

The web version (this site and itch.io) does not display ads.

In-App Purchases

The native app offers optional purchases ("Remove Ads", "PortMaster Pro"). These transactions are processed entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play — we do not store your payment information.

Children

PortMaster is rated 4+ / Everyone and is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Third-party links

Games link back to ljennings11.itch.io. That site is subject to itch.io's own privacy policy.
